# Welcome to Cronozen

Cronozen is a **multi-tenant SaaS operating system** designed for center-based businesses across 7 verticals:

- Rehabilitation
- Welfare
- Education
- Pharmacy
- Commerce
- Mentoring
- Interior

## What Cronozen Does

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Multi-Tenant Platform" icon="building">
    Each center operates as an isolated tenant with its own data, members, and configuration.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Actor-Based Auth" icon="users">
    Role-based access with ADMIN, INSTRUCTOR, PARENT, and CHILD actors.
  </Card>
  <Card title="DPU (Decision Proof)" icon="shield-check">
    SHA-256 hash chain for audit-ready decision tracking and governance.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI Automation" icon="robot">
    Workflow engine with AI-powered scheduling, reporting, and operations.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Ecosystem

Cronozen operates as a **hub-and-spoke** architecture:

| Service | Domain | Role |
|---------|--------|------|
| **OPS** | cronozen.com | Hub — SSO, payments, tenant master, DPU |
| **LMS** | learn.cronozen.com | Learning engine, compliance |
| **CMS** | blog.cronozen.com | Blog, manuals, content |
| **ERP** | erp.cronozen.com | Business management |
